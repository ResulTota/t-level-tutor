ESP - Pre-Task

What Is the Pre-Task?

The Pre-Task for the ESP introduces you to the business sector the ESP will revolve around (e.g: Retail, Real Estate, Fast Food etc.) which is useful as it can give you an idea of why the company might use digital tools/technologies.

  • Access the internet
  • Talk with other students
     Generally, you will be in teams of six, however you can still communicate with those outside of your group.
  • Make notes about your research
     However you Cannot bring these notes into the other exams.

The Pre-Task will outline the goals & requirements of the solution/system and recommend different tools/technologies you could research that are commonly used in the sector outlined.

You are not expected to submit anything for the Pre-Task.

Marks?

The Pre-Task is worth 0 marks.
It is not assessed and only exists so that you have time to understand the business context.

Time?

You will have a week for the Pre-Task.
 However, it is advised you spend around 6 hours on the task.

Tips

  • Use your time wisely to research the sector.
  • Collaborate with your team for broader perspectives.
  • Make clear notes, but remember you can't bring them into the exam.
  • Focus on understanding the goals and requirements.
