ESP - Task 4b: Reflection

What Is Task 4b?

Task 4b is a reflection activity where you review your work from Task 4a,
You will consider:

  • How well your code meets the requirements of the brief
  • Any challenges you faced during development and how you overcame them
  • What you would do differently if you were to undertake a similar task in the future
  • Potential improvements or additional features that could enhance the solution
Note: Task 4b is similar to the Rationale from Task 1.

You will write your reflection in Microsoft Word.

Marks

Task 4b is worth 9 marks.
The marks are split into 2 parts:

  • Programming outcomes - 6 marks
  • Future Developments - 3 marks

Time

You will have 1.5 hours to complete this task.

Tips

  • Use samples/ Screenshots from your work.
