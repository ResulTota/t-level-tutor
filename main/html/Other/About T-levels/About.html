About The T-Level

Assesments

ESP:

In May next year, you will sit The ESP.

  • It is made up of 5 tasks that will take place over a 2 week window.
  • In total, the ESP will last 14hrs 30mins.
    • Task 1: Planning A Project - 3 Hours
    • Task 2: Debugging Existing Code - 3 Hours
    • Task 3: Designing A Solution - 3 Hours
    • Task 4a: Developing A Solution - 4 Hours
    • Task 4b: Reflection - 1.5 Hours

  • It is worth 100 Marks which is 40% of your final grade for year 12.
  • You will be graded from A* to E based on your score out of 100.

Core Papers:

In June next year, you will sit 2 core papers.

  • Each paper will last 2hrs 15mins.
  • Each paper is worth 90 Marks which is 30% of your final grade for year 12.
  • The papers will cover the following topics:
    • Paper 1: Digital Analysis, Legislation & Emerging Issues
      • CA1: Problem Solving
      • CA2: Programming
      • CA3: Emerging Issues
      • CA4: Legislation and Regulatory Requirements

    • Paper 2: The Business Environment
      • CA5: Business
      • CA6: Data
      • CA7: Digital Environments
      • CA8: Security

  • Your grade for the papers will come from combining the marks.
  • You will be graded from A* to E based on your score out of 180.

Importance:

These assesments make up your grade for year 12

  • You will be graded from A* to E based on your score out of 280.
  • This is half your overall grade for the course.
  • Based on your grade, you may be capped as to what grade you can get in year 13.
    • If you get an A or below, you Cannot get a Distinction* overall.
    • If you get a D or below, you Cannot get a Cannot get a Distinction or a Distincton* overall.
  • That being said, you can retake at different points if you need too.
    • however, it is highly recommended you try your best first time round to avoid unnecessary stress in year 13
